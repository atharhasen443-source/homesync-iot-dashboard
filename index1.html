Project Objective

HomeSync is a centralized IoT smart home management system designed to provide seamless control and monitoring of all connected smart home devices through an intuitive web interface.

Key Features

Real-time Synchronization

Live updates and control of all connected devices with minimal latency.

Responsive Design

Optimized for all devices including desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Secure Architecture

Five-layer security model to protect your home and data.

Energy Efficient

Smart energy management to reduce consumption and costs.
